## 核心採用來源

### 1. 富士康、全球生產與中國勞動階級

- Mark Selden, *The politics of global production: Apple, Foxconn and China's new working class*, Asia-Pacific Journal: Japan Focus  
  https://apjjf.org/2013/11/32/mark-selden/3981/article

用途：
- 富士康崛起的結構背景
- 台灣資本 / 中國人口紅利 / 全球代工體系
- Apple / Foxconn / 中國勞工的政治經濟脈絡

### 2. 富士康崛起與勞工處境

- Jenny Chan, *Foxconn's Rise and Labor's Fall in Global China*, PolyU Institutional Repository  
  https://ira.lib.polyu.edu.hk/bitstream/10397/88559/1/Chan_NOV2020_American_Affairs.pdf

用途：
- 富士康商業模式與勞工處境
- 「崛起不是黑科技，而是結構性套利」的論述基礎

### 3. 軍事化管理與全控機構

- SOMO, *Workers as Machines: Military Management in Foxconn*  
  https://www.somo.nl/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/military-management-in-Foxconn.pdf

用途：
- 軍訓式規訓
- 廠區式全控機構
- 宿舍、食堂、工作、休閒一體化管理
- 公開羞辱、檢討文化等管理特徵

### 4. Apple / Foxconn 低成本現實

- China Labor Watch, *Analyzing Labor Conditions of Pegatron and Foxconn: Apple's Low-Cost Reality*  
  https://chinalaborwatch.org/analyzing-labor-conditions-of-pegatron-and-foxconn-apples-low-cost-reality/

用途：
- 勞動條件
- 工資、加班、低成本模式
- 作為工時與剝削結構的輔助來源

### 5. 富士康機器人與成本推估

- New Venture Research, *The Foxconn Robots of the Future – Human or Machine?*  
  https://newventureresearch.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/The-Foxconn-Robots.pdf

用途：
- 機器人導入成本
- 勞動力成本與機器替代的產業計算邏輯
- 「人類在成本表上更划算」的背景

注意：
- 文中的 `$3,750 / 年` 比較接近工人成本估算，不宜直接寫成「Foxconn 工人年薪」。

### 6. 富士康工資與機器人計畫的新聞脈絡

- ABC News, *Foxconn Has a Million Workers, Plans a Million Robots*  
  https://abcnews.com/Technology/foxconn-chinese-computer-giant-plans-million-robots-years/story?id=14204282

用途：
- 2011 年百萬機器人大軍脈絡
- 工人平均月薪約 `290 美元 / 月` 的新聞佐證
- 薪資調升、工人處境與機器人計畫的新聞背景

### 7. 富士康機器人精度不足的二手科技報導

- ExtremeTech, *Foxconn's robotic workforce isn't precise enough to assemble iPhones*  
  https://www.extremetech.com/electronics/195556-foxconns-robotic-workforce-isnt-precise-enough-to-assemble-iphones

- HotHardware, *Foxconn's First Generation Foxbots Aren't Accurate Enough For Apple*  
  https://hothardware.com/news/foxconns-first-generation-foxbots-arent-accurate-enough-for-apple

- PhoneArena, *Apple not pleased with Foxconn's robots-at least for now*  
  https://www.phonearena.com/news/Apple-not-pleased-with-Foxconns-robots-at-least-for-now_id63560

用途：
- Foxbot 精度不足
- `0.05mm vs 0.02mm` 這類技術細節的交叉佐證

### 8. 機器人取代工人敘事的商業科技報導

- Quartz, *Here's how the plan to replace the humans who make iPhones with robots...*  
  https://qz.com/1312079/iphone-maker-foxconn-is-churning-out-foxbots-to-replace-its-human-workers

用途：
- 機器人取代工人敘事
- 產業界如何包裝自動化計畫
