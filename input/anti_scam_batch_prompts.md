# style
```
Style: Warm editorial health-education infographic in a hand-drawn illustration + clean layout hybrid. Inspired by Taiwanese hospital patient-education pamphlets — friendly, readable, trustworthy. Modern wabi-sabi editorial feel with generous whitespace.
Dimensions: 1:1 square, 1080×1080px.

Background: Warm off-white #FAF7F2 (full bleed, slight paper texture feel — NOT pure white).

Illustration style: Soft hand-drawn line-art with flat color fills. Lines are 1.5–2px, slightly imperfect/organic (not mechanical). Scene objects (phone, shield, ATM, lock, chart, etc.) are drawn in the same hand-drawn style. All illustrations are decorative accents — they do NOT replace text content.
IMPORTANT — human figures: Any person silhouette must be completely anonymous — no age, no gender, no ethnicity, no occupation indicators. Use simple featureless blob/circle-head silhouettes only. Scam victims and targets come from all walks of life; never imply a specific demographic.

Color palette:
  - Primary green: #2D6A4F (forest green) — title blocks, section headers, badges, primary labels
  - Accent red: #BC4A3C (terracotta / warm red) — danger, prohibition, alert markers
  - Amber yellow: #F2C14E — CTA highlights, key callout numbers, step markers
  - Warm teal: #52A399 — secondary labels, divider accents
  - Text primary: #2C2416 (warm near-black, very high contrast)
  - Text secondary: #5C4F3D (warm medium brown)
  - Text caption: #8C7B6A (warm gray-brown)
  - Card fill: #FFFFFF with warm shadow rgba(44,36,22,0.07), blur 10px, y+3px
  - Accent bar fill: use primary/accent colors at full saturation for header bars

Typography:
  - All body text minimum 18px equivalent — large-print friendly for elderly readers.
  - Slide titles: ExtraBold, 36–44px, color #2C2416 or white on colored bar.
  - Section labels: Bold, 20px, #2D6A4F.
  - Body / list text: Medium, 18–20px, #2C2416.
  - Captions: Regular, 13–14px, #8C7B6A.
  - Font: Traditional Chinese humanist sans-serif (Noto Sans TC or similar).

Title / section bar style: Rounded-rectangle solid color bar (radius 8px) containing white ExtraBold label + optional small hand-drawn icon to the left. Primary green #2D6A4F for main titles; terracotta #BC4A3C for warnings; amber #F2C14E (dark text) for callouts.

Icon style: Solid flat-color icons inside perfectly round circles (1:1 aspect ratio — never oval). Circle diameter ~52px. White icon on colored circle. Colors: green #2D6A4F (safe/info), red #BC4A3C (danger), amber #F2C14E (action, use dark icon color #2C2416 for contrast), teal #52A399 (secondary).
STRICT RULE: Icon circles must be geometrically perfect circles. Every circle element throughout the design (icon badges, step markers, pagination dots) must have equal width and height — never oval or ellipse shaped.

List rows: Icon circle bullet + text, laid directly on the slide background or inside ONE containing card. NEVER wrap list rows in an additional inner card or border box — no nested cards, no box-in-box layouts.

Hand-drawn accent elements (used sparingly as decoration):
  - Wavy or sketchy underlines beneath key phrases
  - Small hand-drawn stars ✦, leaf sprigs, or bracket decorations around callout numbers
  - Thin hand-drawn divider lines (slightly wobbly, not ruler-straight) between sections
  - Small hand-drawn character illustrations placed at corners or beside key content (e.g. elderly person looking at phone, person raising hand to stop, person thinking)

Series badge: Forest green #2D6A4F rounded pill, white Bold text "防詐教育", top-left corner every slide.
No pagination dots. Do not render any dot indicators or progress indicators at the bottom.
AI watermark: Absolute bottom-right — tiny thin-stroke rectangle "AI Generated", ~10px, #8A8F9E at 55% opacity.
```
